Product Details and Conditions

Applicant’s qualification
  • Permanent officer of any company or private-sector organization.
  • Minimum monthly income of 15,000 Baht.
  • At least 1 year of working experience with the company.
  • Not over 60 years old.
  • Having salary account with Krungthai Bank.
Credit limit
  • Up to 5 times the monthly income but not over 1 million Baht.
Loan period
  • 5 years.
Repayment condition
  • Pay capital with interest.
  • The Bank directly deducts money from the salary account (Auto Debit)
Required documents and evidences for loan application
  • Copies of personal ID card, house registration and employee ID card (if any)
  • Original copy of income certifying letter or latest salary slip or other original copy of evidences stating reliable income.
  • 6-month-backward bank statement is required. In case of a new customer with less-than-6-month of net salary transaction in the account, the salary account of other banks may be submitted for consideration.
  • In case that the applicant applies for refinance purpose, the latest document/ statement of personal loan or credit card; or other documents having the same kind of information are required.
