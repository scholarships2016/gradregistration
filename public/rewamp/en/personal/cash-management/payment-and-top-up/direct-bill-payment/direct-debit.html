Service Features
Direct Debit service is provided through three channels:
  • KTB Branch - simply fill in the application form and attach your ID card’s copy.
  • ATM - apply by entering Comp. Code and Reference Code at ATM
  • KTB netbank - log onto KTB netbank and click Other Service go to - Direct Debit Menu to select the company.
