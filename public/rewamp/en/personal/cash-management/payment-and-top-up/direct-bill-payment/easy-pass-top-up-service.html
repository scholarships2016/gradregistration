Payment & Top Up

Easy-Pass Top Up

All Convenience ways for revenue tax payment KTB Counter Services.
HIGHLIGHT
Service features
Easy
You can choose to top up your Easy Pass via 3 KTB’s channels
Save time
You can choose the perfect channel that fit for you either KTB netbank or ATM/ADM machine nationwide
No Top-up fees
The service at every channel is available for top-up amount of 500 – 5,000 Baht. You may choose the top-up amount at 500 Baht interval, starting from 500 Baht, 1,000 Baht, 1,500 Baht, … to 5,000 Baht

Terms and Conditions

You can choose to top up your Easy Pass via 3 KTB’s channels:
  • KTB netbank
  • KTB ATM
  • KTB ADM machine nationwide
    • The service at every channel is available for top-up amount of 500 – 5,000 Baht. You may choose the top-up amount at 500 Baht interval, starting from 500 Baht, 1,000 Baht, 1,500 Baht, … to 5,000 Baht.
Easy Pass Top-up via KTB netbank
For KTB netbank members
Instruction:
  1. Go to www.ktb.co.th and select KTB netbank.
  2. Enter your user ID and password to log in.
  3. Select “Payee”.
  4. Select “Add Payee” by clicking “Transportation”, select “Expressway Authority of Thailand (Easy Pass Top-up)” and rename the payee. Then enter 10-digit S/N number on the back of Easy Pass.
  5. Select Product/service payment transaction.
  6. Select the payee from the list that represents Expressway Authority of Thailand (Easy Pass Top-up) as specified.
  7. Select a bank account, enter the top-up amount and click “OK”.
  8. Verify the payment information on the screen and click “Confirm”.
  9. The transaction is complete.
  10. * You are required to add payee (step 4) when using Easy Pass top-up service via KTB netbank for first time. The added payee will then appear in your “My Pay List” on KTB netbank
Instruction:
  1. Go to m.ktb.co.th
  2. Enter your user ID and password to log in.
  3. Select “Pay Bill/Top up”.
  4. Select “My Pay List”.
  5. Select your bank account and the payee from the list that represents Expressway Authority of Thailand (Easy Pass Top-up) as specified on KTB netbank.
  6. Enter the top-up amount and click “Submit”.
  7. The transaction is complete.
Easy Pass Top-up via KTB ATM machine
For all KTB card members whose cards are linked with KTB accounts (saving or current account)
Instruction:
  1. nsert card / enter your password.
  2. Select “Easy Pass Top-up Service”.
  3. Select “the account type”.
  4. Enter 10-digit S/N number on the back of the card, enter the top-up amount, then click “Correct”.
  5. Receive the transaction slip (if the slip is not available, you can confirm to proceed the transaction without the slip)
