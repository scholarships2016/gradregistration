Tax Payment

All Convenience ways for revenue tax payment KTB Counter Services.

Service features
Worry-free with Tax
Krungthai Bank is now offering customers the Convenience ways for revenue tax payment
Only one bank
KTB is the first and only bank in Thailand responsible for tax form submitting

Terms and Conditions

All Convenience ways for revenue tax payment:
  • KTB Counter Services nationwide.
  • KTB ATMs nationwide.
  • KTB netbank (Individual)
  • KTB Corporate Online (Juristic Person)
  • Page 2 Page (Company/Organization Website)
  • Direct Debit (Automatic Account Debiting)
  • KTB is the first and only bank in Thailand responsible for tax form submitting (please see... Tax Payment Manual)
Payment:
Revenue Tax
Internet payment (KTB netbank ,KTB Corporate Banking, Page 2 Page) using following information:
Ref : Citizen / TAX ID (ID card number/ Tax ID number) and
Ref : 15 digits of Control Code or Reference number
Excise Tax
KTB bank branch nationwide or via Internet (KTB netbank, KTB Corporate Online, Page 2 Page) using following information:
Ref1 : Payment Ref. number
Ref2 : Tax ID number
Local and Council Tax
Submit your tax receipt to KTB Counter Service nationwide
Local Tax for Bangkok resident
  • Submit your tax receipt to KTB Counter Service, pay via KTB ATM or via Internet (Page 2 Page)
  • ชำระได้ทุกบัตร ทุกธนาคาร รับใบเสร็จทันที (ภาษีโรงเรือนและที่ดิน / ภาษีบำรุงท้องที่ / ภาษีป้าย)
ค่าธรรมเนียม
  • บัตร ATM & Visa Debit ของธนาคารกรุงไทย ค่าธรรมเนียม 10 บาท / รายการ
  • บัตร Debit / Credit ประเภท Visa / Master ของทุกธนาคาร ค่าธรรมเนียม 1% ของยอดชำระเงิน / รายการ
Tax Payment Available:
  • Label Tax, Property/ Land Tax, Local Maintenance Tax
  • Social Security Office
  • Car/Motorcycle Tax Department of Land Transport
    • Submit tax receipt to KTB Counter Service nationwide or, pay via KTB ATM, Internet (KTB netbank, KTB Corpoarte Online, Page 2 Page) or via KTB Direct Debit.
  • Car Tax Submitting Available Online
    • Personal car, van, and pick-up registered not over seven years.
    • Personal motorcycle registered not over five years.
    • Not more than three years outstanding tax.
    • In case of delayed of payment, tax receipt will be calculated automatically by tax system.
    • Tax payment online will be available three months in advance before the end date stated on tax receipt.
    • Note: For Personal car, van, and pick-up registered not over seven years, and personal motorcycle registered not over five years, tax payment can be done at Auto Check Center nationwide.
  • Import/Export Duty
    • KTB’s 24 hours online service available for import and export duty via two channels:
    • Auto Debit: KTB will automatically debit payment in your account after receiving debiting information from Thai Customs Department.
    • Pre-Approve*: Tax payment information can be checked before debiting transaction via KTB Corporate Online.
