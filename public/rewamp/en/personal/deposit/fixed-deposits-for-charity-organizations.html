Fixed-term Deposits for Charity Organizations

This is a time deposit product designed for non-profit juristic persons.
HIGHLIGHT
Unique Features
Interest is paid monthly
to allow depositors needing the income to meet their financial obligations.
INTERESTS
RATE
Interest rates per Bank’s announcement on the day the deposits are made
See Interest Rates

Product details and Conditions

Product Feature:
  • Long-term fixed deposits with the choices of 48- and 60-month maturity terms.
  • Minimum Baht 10,000 per deposit and no part withdrawal.
  • Interest is transferred every month to one of the designated accounts (savings/ current account) per customers’ instruction.
  • Deposits under three months will earn no interest. However, interest paid earlier will be deducted from the capital amount of deposits.
  • Should a withdrawal take place three months after the account was opened but prior to maturity, interest paid will be based on the rate paid to savings account for the actual period of the deposit and in accordance with the deposit term specified on the day the account was opened. Interests are subject to withholding tax and surplus interest paid will be deducted from the fixed deposit account.
  • On maturity, the deposits will be transferred automatically to the designated account (three-month fixed-term account/ savings account/ current account) per customers’ instruction.
Documents required (Temples, mosques, religious shrines)
  • ID proof of authorized signatory to order payments on behalf of the temples, mosques and religious shrines.
  • Establishment or Status Certifying Permit (in the case of temples)
  • Proof of Abbot Appointment (in the case of temples)
  • Letter of Intent endorsed by authorized signature(s) and executive seal for the opening of the account along with details of designated authorized signatory and terms of payment.
  • Proofs of tariff/interest tax exemption (if any).
Documents required (Associations, foundations)
  • ID proof of authorized signatory to order payments on behalf of the association/foundation.
  • Association/Foundation Establishment Permit.
  • Association/Foundation Statute.
  • Commercial Registration Certificate (if any)
  • Committee Meeting Report for the Association/Foundation authorizing the opening of the account with the Bank or Letter of Intent endorsed by authorized signature(s) and executive seal to open the account along with details of designated authorized signatory and terms of payment.
  • Proofs of tariff/interest tax exemption (if any)
Documents required (Clubs and other organizations)
  • ID proof of authorized signatory to order payments on behalf of the said organization(s).
  • Certificate of Registration issued by the Interior Ministry or other Ministries or related state agencies.
  • Establishment Permit of the Organization.
  • Organization’s Statute.
  • Commercial Registration Certificate (if any).
  • Proof of appointment or authorization for the opening of the account with the Bank (if any).
  • Committee Meeting Report for the Club/Organization authorizing the opening of the account with the Bank or Letter of Intent endorsed by authorized signature(s) and executive seal to open the account along with details of designated authorized signatory and terms of payment.
  • Proofs of tariff/interest tax exemption (if any).
Documents required (Private Education Institutions)
  • ID proof of authorized signatory to order payments on behalf of the education institution.
  • Proof of approval for the establishment of education institution with the Education Ministry or Ministry of University Affairs.
  • Committee Meeting Report authorizing the education institution to open the account with the Bank or Letter of Intent endorsed by authorized signature(s) and executive seal for the opening of the account along with details of designated authorized signatory and terms of payment.
Documents required (Condominium Juristic Persons)
  • Proof of Condominium Juristic Person Registration and the name of its manager.
  • Condominium Juristic Person’s Tax ID (if any).
  • Condominium Juristic Person Committee Meeting Report indicating the intent to open the account and details of designated authorized signatory and terms of payment.
  • ID proof of manager of the Condominium Juristic Person.
  • ID proof of the authorized signatory to order payments.
