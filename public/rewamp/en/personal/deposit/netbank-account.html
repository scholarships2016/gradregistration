Product details and Conditions

Net deposit account (no book account) which are
  • Net Savings the primary netbank saving account for daily.
  • Net Extra the extra saving account that offers 0.5% higher interest rate than Net Saving (minimum balance is 10,000 baht per monthand withdrawal must not be over than 4 times per month)
  • Net Fixed is fixed deposit account higher interest rate than ordinary fixed account. NetFlexible Fix is day term deposit which allows you to select deposit term by yourself (bank will launch products and promotions constantly)
