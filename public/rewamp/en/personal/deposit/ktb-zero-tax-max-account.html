Product details and Conditions

Product details
  • Minimum requirement for account opening
    • saving period of 24 months   1,000 – 25,000 Baht
    • saving period of 36 months   1,000 – 16,500 Baht
    • saving period of 48 months   1,000 – 12,500 Baht
  • Equivalent monthly deposit amount to the initial account opening.
  • Maximum of 2 stops are allowed for deposit period.
  • Interest shall be calculated everyday and subject to the Bank’s announcement but such interest shall be paid on maturity date and transfer to saving account / current account as specified to the Bank.
  • No interest shall be paid for less than 3-month deposit.
  • For withdrawal after 3 months of deposit or not able to comply with deposit terms, interest shall be calculated based on saving account interest rate and subject to interest tax.
  • - At the maturity of the deposit, the capital fund shall be transferred to the saving, current, the 3-month fixed deposit account as specified to the bank.
  • Depositor shall have the right of tax exemption from deposit interest for only one account (including the same account type of every bank)
