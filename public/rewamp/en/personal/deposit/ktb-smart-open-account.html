Unique Features

Product details and Conditions

ลักษณะของบริการ
Open an account with ATM card easily and instantly at one stop service. With KTB easy service you can open account with your ID card (Smart Card or Magnetic Card) without filing in the form for opening account. KTB system will scan customers’ ID card and automatically print out the account opening request form with EDC (Electronic Data Capture) machine. Also you can open ATM Card from the EDC (Electronic Data Capture) machine instantly.
